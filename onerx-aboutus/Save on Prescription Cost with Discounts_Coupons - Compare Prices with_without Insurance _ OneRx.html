    About Us

    We are on a mission to reduce health care costs for Americans by putting
    real-time prescription savings at everyone’s fingertips.

    How? We make it easy for the almost 70% of Americans who take prescription drugs to access comprehensive information on drug-specific co-pays, and accurately compare costs & money-saving options.

    MEET THE TEAM

    Our Story

    For years, we’ve created innovative prescription programs to help companies save money. As we watched drug costs continue to skyrocket, it got us thinking: How can we use our expertise to help all Americans save money? The result is OneRx.

    To learn more about us, check out our parent company site, Truveris.com.

    WE BELIEVE

    • Transparency, empowerment & simplicity are the keys to saving money on prescription drugs.

    • Being proactive is important in purchasing prescriptions.

    • Access to value should be
      fast, easy & free.
